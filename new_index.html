  • Hi(story)


    • 2000

    • Genesis


      Our engagement with data storage, goes back over a decade, to the year 2000, when The Datalifecycle Company was incorporated as an independent system builder of data storage solutions.

      Using hardware and software building blocks from a number of vendors across the world, we have been building independent data storage solutions spanning the lifecycle of data; from storage, to protection, archival and disposal.

    • Commitment & Focus


      Over several years, Datalifecycle's storage solutions have found acceptance with a wide range of customers across different verticals including manufacturing, financial services, media & entertainment, government, IT services, etc.

      However, by the end of the decade, it was aparent, that in order to deliver durable customer value we needed to be truly independent, and build our own storage platform.

    • 2012

    • Incubation


      Sometime in 2012, we began evaluating a number of candidate open source projects and commodity hardware designs, that could serve as the base for our data storage platform.

    • 2014

    • Fractalio Data is born


      In 2014, this initiative was forked into a new company - Fractalio Data.

    • Integralstor


      Hooray! The first version of IntegralStor for unified and scale out storage is ready....and, we have our first customers!

    • Dell OEM Partnership


      Fractalio becomes a Dell OEM storage partner and brings the rock solid hardware quality and instant geographically widerspread support reach to all our customers. We expand our customer reach and use cases significantly.

    Fractalio Data was conceived in response to the challenge of containing and reliably managing the explosion of electronic data that IT enabled organizations of all sizes are experiencing.

    Fractalio Data is led by a group of individuals with experience spanning technology, engineering and business, who started off over a decade ago as vendor-independent system integrators of data storage solutions, and were among the few Indian companies to design and deliver solutions addressing the full spectrum of “Datalifecycle” requirements, from data storage, through archival, retrieval and elimination; from in-premise to the cloud.

    In addition to, and of equal importance as our data storage initiative, as individuals and as a group, we're interested in a wider spectrum of technology - its role, influence and impact on sustenance, convenience and leisure, and consequently then, on livelihoods and the nature and role of work in society. In more concrete terms, we're interested in harnessing technology to address local needs towards sustainable living in the areas of food, water, energy security & shelter, and strive to provide an environment that supports explorations in these areas. So, our data storage business is but one dimension, in a much broader set of motivations, and serves as an enabler to facilitate these explorations.
